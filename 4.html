Calculator Logic

All written logic is shown below the calculator as requested.

  • discount = max(scholarship percentage, waiver percentage)
  • Only the maximum one counts, not both. If both are 25%, then 25% counts. If one is 25% and another is 50%, then 50% counts.
  • fresh credits = Total Credits - Retake Credits - FYDP Credits
  • fresh fee = (fresh credits × Per Credit Fee) × (100 - discount)%
  • retake credit fee = (retake credits × Per Credit Fee) / 2
  • FYDP credit does not fall into scholarship and waiver, so it is calculated separately.
  • fydp fee = fydp credits × Per Credit Fee
  • Registration Fee and Extras do not fall into scholarship and waiver, so they are directly added to total fee.
  • Total Fee = fresh fee + retake credit fee + fydp fee + Registration Fee + Extras
  • The Final Fee is displayed to the user.
Payment in Installment (from payable amount)
  • Type 1: 40% · 30% · 30% when you have not paid anything while registration.
  • Type 2: 40% · 30% · 30% when you already paid your registration fee.
  • 1st installment = 40%, 2nd installment = 30%, 3rd installment = 30%
